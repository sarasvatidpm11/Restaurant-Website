About Us

In December 2023, the Pi7u Bali Restaurant culinary business opened with great love for Balinese culinary culture. With the vision of becoming the main destination that presents an authentic Balinese experience through distinctive flavors and cultural beauty in every dish we serve at Pi7u Bali Restaurant. Pi7u Bali Restaurant is located on Jln. Samratulangi No.14, Singaraja, Buleleng, Bali. Open from 09.00 WITA - 22.00 WITA.
