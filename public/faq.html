FAQs

What are the best-selling foods and drinks at Pi7u Bali Restaurant?

The best-selling foods and drinks at Pi7u Bali Restaurant are Babi Guling and Es Kelapa Muda.

Does Pi7u Bali Restaurant have a special food or drink menu that specializes in certain regions or cultures that can be recommended?

Of course! Here are our recommendations:

Snack
Klepon Traditional round cake filled with liquid brown sugar, and served with grated coconut.

Main Course
Ayam Betutu: A chicken dish cooked with Balinese spices that is rich in flavor and usually cooked in a long process.

Drink
Daluman: A traditional drink made from grass jelly leaf extract served with brown sugar and coconut milk, giving a fresh and typical Balinese taste.
