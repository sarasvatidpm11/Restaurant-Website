About us

History

In December 2023, the Pi7u Bali Restaurant culinary business opened with great love for Balinese culinary culture. Rooted in the rich culinary heritage of the Island of the Gods, this business was born from a desire to provide customers with an authentic Balinese experience. In an atmosphere full of the warmth and beauty of Balinese culture, this business offers a variety of typical food and drink dishes, inspired by the rich culinary traditions in every corner of the island of Bali. Through authentic flavors and the best local ingredients, this business is determined to be a bridge for culinary lovers to enjoy traditional Balinese delights.

Vision

To be the main destination that presents an authentic Balinese experience through distinctive flavors and cultural beauty in every dish we serve at Pi7u Bali Restaurant.

Mision

Menghadirkan kuliner khas Bali, memadukan tradisi otentik dengan bahan-bahan premium, memberikan layanan luar biasa, dan mewujudkan esensi hangat keramahtamahan Bali.
